# Self-Reinforcing Money Loop — RalphWorkflow / HireAegis

## The Core Insight

Two assets:
1. **RalphWorkflow** — nobody pays for a CLI. But: people pay for RESULTS from using it
2. **HireAegis** — companies pay for efficient, evidence-based technical hiring

**The loop:** Content proves value → trust builds → people pay for the SaaS → revenue funds better content → cycle accelerates
