Ralph Workflow is a free and open-source composable loop framework and AI orchestrator for developers doing ambitious software work that needs a structured workflow instead of a chat session. The core difference is that it keeps a simple loop at the center, then composes planning, implementation, and verification so longer unattended runs end in something bounded and reviewable instead of another confident summary.
