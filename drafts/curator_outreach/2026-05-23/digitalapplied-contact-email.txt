Ralph Workflow is a free and open-source workflow layer for autonomous coding: a composable loop framework and AI orchestrator for developers and technical teams who need more structure than a chat session.

Why it may be a useful inclusion in this comparison:
- it sits at the workflow/orchestration layer rather than the editor/model layer
- it is built for work that is too big to babysit but too risky to trust blindly
- it composes planning, implementation, verification, and handoff so unattended runs end in something reviewable
- it gives open-source-first teams a Codeberg-primary option that can use the default workflow as-is or be extended
