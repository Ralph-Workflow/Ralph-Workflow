I noticed your awesome-ai-coding-tools list is actively curating coding agents and workflow-layer tools, and I think Ralph Workflow would fit well there.

Ralph Workflow is a free and open-source composable loop framework and AI orchestrator for developers who want a structured workflow instead of a chat session. The useful difference is that it composes planning, development, and verification into one repo-native loop so longer unattended runs end in finished, tested code that is ready to review instead of another confident summary.
