Hi Wix Engineering team — your recent post “From Co-Pilot to Full Automation: How Wix Is Embedding AI Agents Across an Engineering Org at Scale” does a good job separating cloud agents, coding-agent harnesses, and full-computer agents.

Ralph Workflow might be a useful open-source reference for that conversation from the workflow layer side. It’s the operating system for autonomous coding: a free and open-source composable loop framework and AI orchestrator for developers and technical teams who need a workflow instead of a chat session.

The core difference is that it keeps a simple loop at the center, then composes planning, implementation, verification, and review around it so the morning-after result is inspectable instead of just “agent said done.”

If you publish another roundup or follow-up on agentic coding systems at engineering-org scale, it may be a strong fit as a workflow-layer example.
